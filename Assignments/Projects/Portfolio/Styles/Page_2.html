Dreams of Lust. Dreams of War. Dreams of the Gloomy Castle.

Pretty Like a Veil is a novel about Suicide in COVID era America. It is about American fatalism, the things we do in the pursuit of happiness that are, in reality, in the pursuit of self-destruction. It is about a strange dark castle that looks like a jagged black asteroid. It is about a charming man named Lacidius…or Ladicius as he sometimes calls himself by mistake.

He is the host of the castle. He speaks in poetic riddles.

Now go onward…

Follow the trail into Lacidius’s castle. It’s like H.H. Holmes meets Hotel California...
